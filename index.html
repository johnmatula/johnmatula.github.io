I’m John Matula. I’m a human.
I’m a life-long musician and designer,
starting with piano and traffic signs(!) as a kid.
My passion is systems: the way they get made,
how they’re used, and the benefits they offer us.
I have 14 years’ design experience delivering
SaaS product designs, long-lasting websites
and apps, and technical documentation.
Complex environments are my specialty —
whether that’s on stage or in the office.

I take on design and talent roles
on a contract and temporary basis via

Otherwise, I’m probably repotting a plant
or making another playlist thumbnail.
