I’m John Matula and I’m a human. I am a
life‑long learner of design and technology.
My passion is systems: how they are built,
the way they are used, and the utility they offer.
I have led design teams for six years, guiding
and delivering SaaS product mockups, user flows,
branded websites, and detailed documentation.
Complex environments are my specialty.

I take on Product Design
and Website Design and Audit projects
through JM Creative, my freelance business.
On a contract basis, I also happily accept
Piano, Acting, and Vocal Performance work.

Outside of that, I’m probably
taking a hike, catching up on Drag Race España,
or making too many playlist thumbnails.
