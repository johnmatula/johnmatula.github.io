Hello! I’m John, a human —
a musician, designer, and technologist.
Systems have captivated me my whole life,
from the piano and traffic signs to DOS.

As a technical UX designer for 15 years,
I’ve helped people reach goals by designing
solutions for systems. My work has spanned
industries and responsibilities, and I am
most energized when I help others directly.

I work with clients to create blended designs:
mobile apps, real-world interior designs,
and the processes that combine them into
thoughtful user experiences. This blended
approach lets me embrace complexity,
supporting small community and nonprofit
groups with work that strives to amplify
the best of our boroughs.
