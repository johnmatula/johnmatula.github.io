Hello! I’m John.
I’ve been a musician my whole life.
I started in kindergarten on the family
electric piano from JCPenney Outlet.

Across two decades of piano playing
and around seven of singing and acting,
I have performed in recitals, services,
contests, and sold‑out houses.
In 2016, I veered fully into tech.
I dialed it back to a healthy balance
and have since resumed talent work —
surprise, past coworkers!

I fit schmaltzy and serious roles alike,
anything that could use the nuance
and grace you only find in us humans.


I serve as an actor and musician to immerse and stir audiences, in groups and on stage.
I also offer talent on a contract basis.
